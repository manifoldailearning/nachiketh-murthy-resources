One Planner

Lessons Learned

Lesson 1
Complexity Kills POCs
Multi-agent systems look impressive in demos. They die in production debugging.
Lesson 2
Simplicity Has Limits
Single agent couldn't handle edge cases. Every new requirement required prompt surgery.
Lesson 3
Hybrid Approaches Win
One planner (routing logic) + Multiple tools (specialized execution) + Clear separation of concerns = Production architecture
